Join us at DMCON 2025, Kerala’s premier DevOps and cloud native conference, happening in Kochi on September 13, 2025. This community-driven event brings together engineers, architects, and practitioners from across the DevOps and cloud native ecosystem.

Be part of the conversation as leading voices, emerging talent, and open source contributors come together for a full day of collaboration, learning, and real-world insights — shaping the future of DevOps in India and beyond.

Get Experience Empowering DevOps Minds, Together United

Experience a premier event brimming with knowledge and excitement as we bring together DevOps enthusiasts, industry experts, and innovators under one roof.
